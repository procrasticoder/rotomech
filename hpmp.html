High Pressure Multistage Pumps


All These Pumps are very rigid and heavy duty specially suitable for industries and other continuous operation jobs. The block of these pumps are separate Unit and the bearing of the pumps can be changed without disturbing the pipe lines. The space for stuffing box is a adequate for easy replacement of gland packing.

High Pressure Multistage Pumps are suitable for water supply and other Clear liquid transferring purpose. These are built as multistage centrifugal pumps. To Increase the total head should an increase in the number of the stages. Pump shaft is guided antifriction bearings at both ends, The standard pumps suction pipe is directed to the left side, and the discharge pipe extends Vertically upward. The antifriction bearings are grease lubricated.
